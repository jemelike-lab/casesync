# CaseSync Training Video Script
**Beatrice Loving Heart (BLH) — CaseSync Portal**
*Estimated runtime: ~11 minutes*

---

## 1. Intro [0:00 – 0:30]

**[VISUAL CUE]** BLH logo fades in on a clean background, then transitions to the CaseSync login screen. Upbeat background music plays softly.

**[NARRATION]**
"Welcome to CaseSync — the case management portal for Beatrice Loving Heart. CaseSync is your central hub for managing clients, tracking deadlines, collaborating with your team, and staying on top of compliance. Whether you're a Supports Planner, Team Manager, or Supervisor, this video will walk you through everything you need to know. Let's get started."

---

## 2. Login & Onboarding [0:30 – 1:30]

**[VISUAL CUE]** Screen shows the CaseSync login page at the URL. Cursor highlights the email and password fields. Screen then shows the "Set your password" onboarding flow, including the progress indicator.

**[NARRATION]**
"To access CaseSync, open your web browser and go to your organization's CaseSync link. Enter the email address your Team Manager or Supervisor used to invite you, then enter your password.

If this is your first time logging in, you'll receive an invitation email. Click the link in that email — it will take you directly to a secure setup page where you'll create your password. Choose something strong: at least eight characters, with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

Once you're logged in, CaseSync may walk you through a brief onboarding checklist — things like confirming your name, your role, and your contact preferences. Complete these steps and you'll land on your Dashboard."

---

## 3. Dashboard Overview [1:30 – 3:30]

**[VISUAL CUE]** Full Dashboard view shown. Camera pans slowly across the screen. Each element gets a brief highlight box as it's mentioned — greeting, alert banner, week strip, client cards.

**[NARRATION]**
"This is your Dashboard — your home base in CaseSync.

At the top, you'll see a personalized greeting with your name and role. Right below that is the **Alert Banner**. If any of your clients have overdue tasks, missed monitoring deadlines, or compliance flags, they'll appear here in amber or red. Pay close attention to this section — it's designed to surface your most urgent priorities at a glance.

Just below the alerts is the **Week Strip** — a seven-day timeline that shows deadlines falling in the next week. Each deadline is color-coded: red means overdue, amber means due soon, and green means on track. Click any deadline chip to jump straight to that client.

Below the week strip, you'll see your **Client Cards**. Each card shows the client's name, their current status, and key deadline indicators. Cards with a colored dot in the corner are flagged for attention.

In the top-right area of the dashboard, you'll find three tools to help you manage your list:

- **Search** — type any part of a client's name to filter instantly
- **Filters** — narrow your view by status, deadline type, or compliance flag
- **Sort** — arrange clients by name, next deadline, or risk level

Use these tools together to zero in on exactly who needs your attention today."

---

## 4. Client Profile [3:30 – 5:30]

**[VISUAL CUE]** Click on a client card. The full client profile page opens. Camera highlights each section of the form as it's mentioned. Shows the Edit button being clicked, fields being updated, and Save being pressed.

**[NARRATION]**
"Click on any client card to open their full profile.

The client profile page is divided into several sections. At the top, you'll see the client's name, Medicaid ID, date of birth, and primary phone number. Below that are fields for their address, emergency contact, and preferred communication method.

In the middle section, you'll find the **Eligibility & Services** panel. This is where you'll see the client's eligibility codes — the specific Medicaid waiver services they're enrolled in, like Community Living Supports or Respite Care. If you're unsure what a code means, hover over it for a description, or ask BLH Bot.

Further down is the **Monitoring & Compliance** section. This shows key dates: the last contact log, the last SPM submission, and the next deadline. Dates highlighted in red are overdue.

To make changes, click the **Edit** button in the top-right corner of the profile. All fields become editable. Use the date pickers to update deadline fields — just click the calendar icon and select the date. When you're done, click **Save** to commit your changes. You'll see a confirmation message at the bottom of the screen.

At the top of the profile, you may also see a yellow **Alert Banner** for this specific client. This could be a compliance warning, an upcoming service authorization expiration, or a note from your Team Manager.

Finally, look for the **AI Summary** button — it's labeled 'Generate Summary' or shows a sparkle icon. Click it to have our AI assistant, BLH Bot, generate a concise summary of the client's current status, recent activity, and any outstanding items. This is especially useful before a visit or team meeting."

---

## 5. BLH Bot [5:30 – 6:30]

**[VISUAL CUE]** The purple floating action button (FAB) in the bottom-right corner pulses. Click it. The BLH Bot chat panel slides open. Show typing a question and getting a response.

**[NARRATION]**
"Meet BLH Bot — your AI assistant, available anytime you need help.

Look for the purple button in the bottom-right corner of your screen. Click it to open the BLH Bot chat panel. You can ask it anything related to your work in CaseSync.

For example, you might ask: 'What's due this week for my clients?' — and BLH Bot will summarize your upcoming deadlines. Or try: 'How do I log a home visit?' — and it will walk you through the steps. You can even ask: 'What does eligibility code CLS mean?' — and it will explain it in plain language.

BLH Bot remembers the context of your conversation, so you can follow up with questions like 'Can you tell me more about that?' without repeating yourself.

BLH Bot is not a replacement for clinical judgment or official BLH policy — but it's a great first stop when you have a quick question or need a refresher. Your Team Manager and Supervisor are always available for escalated questions."

---

## 6. Calendar [6:30 – 7:30]

**[VISUAL CUE]** Navigate to the Calendar page via the top navigation. Show the Month view, then click Week, then Day. Show deadlines appearing as colored chips on the calendar.

**[NARRATION]**
"The Calendar gives you a visual overview of all your deadlines and important dates in one place.

Use the navigation tabs to switch between **Day**, **Week**, and **Month** views. The Month view is great for planning ahead — you can see at a glance which days have heavy deadline loads. The Week view gives you a day-by-day breakdown, and the Day view zooms into a single day's schedule.

Deadline chips on the calendar are color-coded by urgency. Click any chip to see details about that deadline and jump directly to the related client profile.

Use the calendar to plan your work: identify clusters of deadlines, avoid surprises at the end of the month, and make sure your Monthly Monitoring submissions — due by the 15th — are tracked and visible."

---

## 7. Chat [7:30 – 8:30]

**[VISUAL CUE]** Navigate to the Chat page. Show the left sidebar with DMs and channels. Click on a Team Manager name for a DM. Then click on a team channel. Then show a client thread.

**[NARRATION]**
"CaseSync includes a built-in messaging system so you can communicate with your team without leaving the portal.

The Chat page is divided into three areas:

**Direct Messages** — private one-on-one conversations with specific teammates. Click your Team Manager's name to send them a direct message. All conversations are private and secure.

**Team Channels** — group conversations for your whole team or specific groups. Use these to share updates, ask questions, or coordinate on shared priorities. Your Team Manager may post important announcements here.

**Client Threads** — you can have focused conversations attached to a specific client. This keeps client-related discussions organized and easy to find later.

All Chat messages are stored securely within CaseSync. Avoid sharing sensitive client information in chat — use the client profile and documents for official records."

---

## 8. Documents [8:30 – 9:00]

**[VISUAL CUE]** Open a client profile. Scroll to the Documents section. Show the Upload button, a file browser dialog, and a successfully uploaded file appearing in the list.

**[NARRATION]**
"The Documents section in each client profile lets you upload and access important files — assessments, service plans, authorizations, and more.

To upload a document, open the client's profile and scroll to the Documents section. Click **Upload**, choose the file from your computer, and confirm. The file will be securely stored in SharePoint and linked to that client's record. You and your team can access uploaded documents anytime from the client profile.

Keep your document naming consistent — for example: 'ClientLastName_SPM_2025-03.pdf' — so files are easy to find."

---

## 9. Supervisor & Team Manager Features [9:00 – 10:00]

**[VISUAL CUE]** Show the Team page (for Team Managers) and Supervisor dashboard (for Supervisors). Highlight the charts, heatmap, and Admin panel.

**[NARRATION]**
"If you're a Team Manager or Supervisor, you have access to additional tools for managing your team.

**Team Managers** can view a Team Dashboard showing compliance stats for each of their Supports Planners — how many clients each planner manages, how many deadlines are overdue, and overall compliance rates. You can assign clients to specific planners directly from this view, and monitor who may need extra support.

**Supervisors** have access to the full **Supervisor Dashboard**, which includes organization-wide charts, a compliance heatmap, and risk scores across all clients. You can drill down by team, by region, or by individual planner.

Supervisors also have access to the **Admin Panel** — where you can invite new users, change roles, reset passwords, and manage team assignments. The **Audit Log** records every significant action taken in the system, which supports HIPAA compliance and accountability. You can export reports as CSV files for external review or reporting to state agencies."

---

## 10. Notifications [10:00 – 10:30]

**[VISUAL CUE]** Highlight the bell icon in the header. Show the notification dropdown opening, listing several alert types with colored icons.

**[NARRATION]**
"The notification bell in the top-right corner of the header keeps you informed of important events.

Click the bell to see your recent notifications. Types include:
- **Deadline reminders** — when a client deadline is approaching or overdue
- **Compliance alerts** — when a Monthly Monitoring report is due or missed
- **Team messages** — when your Team Manager posts in a channel you follow
- **System updates** — occasional notices about new features or scheduled maintenance

Unread notifications are shown with a red badge on the bell icon. Stay on top of your notifications — they're your early warning system."

---

## 11. Mobile App [10:30 – 11:00]

**[VISUAL CUE]** Show CaseSync open in a mobile browser (iPhone or Android). Show the "Add to Home Screen" prompt. Show the icon appearing on the home screen. Then show the portal running as a full-screen app.

**[NARRATION]**
"CaseSync works on your phone as a Progressive Web App — which means you can install it on your home screen just like a native app, with no app store required.

On iPhone, open CaseSync in Safari, tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen, and select 'Add to Home Screen.' On Android, open CaseSync in Chrome, tap the three-dot menu, and select 'Add to Home Screen' or 'Install App.'

Once installed, CaseSync will appear as an icon on your home screen and launch in full-screen mode — no browser chrome, no distractions. The mobile layout is fully optimized, with a bottom navigation bar for easy one-thumb access to your Dashboard, Chat, Calendar, and more."

---

**[VISUAL CUE]** Return to the Dashboard view. BLH logo fades in. Contact information or help resources briefly appear on screen.

**[NARRATION]**
"That's everything you need to get started with CaseSync. You now know how to navigate your Dashboard, manage client profiles, log contacts, submit Monthly Monitoring, use the Calendar, communicate with your team through Chat, and access documents.

If you ever have a question, your first stop is **BLH Bot** — just click the purple button and ask. For anything that needs a human answer, reach out to your **Team Manager** or **Supervisor** through the Chat feature.

Welcome to the team, and welcome to CaseSync. Let's do great work together for the people we serve."
