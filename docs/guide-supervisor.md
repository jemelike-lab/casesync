## 1. Getting Started

### Account Setup

As a Supervisor, your account is either set up by another Supervisor or the system administrator. You'll receive an invitation email with a secure setup link.

1. Click the **"Set Up Your Account"** link in the invitation email.
2. Create a strong password (8+ characters, mixed case, numbers/symbols).
3. Confirm your password and click **Save Password**.
4. Complete the brief onboarding checklist.

### Your Role and Permissions

As a Supervisor, you have the **highest level of access** in CaseSync:
- View all clients across all teams
- Access every Team Manager's team dashboard
- Access the Supervisor Dashboard with org-wide analytics
- Manage users via the Admin Panel (invite, change roles, deactivate)
- View the Audit Log for HIPAA compliance
- Export org-wide reports as CSV
- Access all Chat channels and threads

### Logging In

1. Go to your organization's CaseSync URL.
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. Click **Sign In**.

## 7. Supervisor Dashboard

The Supervisor Dashboard gives you a real-time, org-wide view of CaseSync activity.

### Accessing the Supervisor Dashboard

Click **Supervisor** in the top navigation bar.

### Overview Panels

| Panel | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Total Clients** | Active client count across the org |
| **Active Planners** | Number of active Supports Planners |
| **SPM Compliance Rate** | % of SPMs submitted on time (this month) |
| **Overdue Items** | Total overdue deadlines across all teams |

### Charts & Analytics

**Compliance by Team**
- Bar chart showing SPM compliance % for each team.
- Click a bar to drill into that team's details.

**Compliance Trend**
- Line chart showing compliance rates over the past 6 months.
- Identify whether compliance is improving or declining.

**Deadline Heatmap**
- A calendar-style heatmap showing deadline density by day.
- Dark squares = many deadlines that day; light squares = few.
- Use this to anticipate high-pressure periods and plan support accordingly.

**Risk Scores**
- Each client has an automated risk score based on:
  - Overdue SPMs
  - Missed contact logs
  - Expiring service authorizations
  - Days since last contact
- Higher scores = higher risk of compliance failure or service disruption.
- Sort the client list by risk score to prioritize your review.

### Team-by-Team Drill-Down

1. From the Supervisor Dashboard, find the **Teams** section.
2. Click any Team Manager's name to see:
   - Their planners and client assignments
   - Individual planner compliance stats
   - Flagged clients within their team
3. Use this to have informed conversations with Team Managers about their team's performance.

### Org-Wide Client List

The Supervisor Dashboard includes a filterable list of all clients in the system:
- Filter by team, planner, risk score, compliance status, or service type
- Click any client to open their profile
- Sort by next deadline, risk score, or last activity

## 8. Admin Panel — Managing Users

The Admin Panel is your tool for managing who has access to CaseSync.

### Accessing the Admin Panel

Click **Admin** in the top navigation bar.

### Inviting a New User

1. In the Admin Panel, click **+ Invite User**.
2. Enter the new user's:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Role (Supports Planner, Team Manager, or Supervisor)
   - Team assignment (for planners and team managers)
3. Click **Send Invitation**.

The user will receive an email with a secure link to set up their account. The link expires after 48 hours — resend if needed.

> 💡 **Tip:** Always confirm the user's email address before sending the invitation. Invitations sent to the wrong address can create security issues.

### Changing a User's Role

1. In the Admin Panel, find the user in the **Users** list.
2. Click their name to open their user profile.
3. Click **Edit Role**.
4. Select the new role from the dropdown.
5. Click **Save**.

The change takes effect immediately — the user's navigation and permissions will update on their next page load.

> ⚠️ **Important:** Changing a user's role can affect their access to client records. Review the implications before making a change:
> - Demoting a Team Manager to Planner removes their team dashboard access
> - Promoting a Planner to Team Manager grants access to their assigned team's data
> - Granting Supervisor access gives full org-wide access — use carefully

### Deactivating a User

When a staff member leaves BLH:
1. In the Admin Panel, find the user.
2. Click their name → click **Deactivate Account**.
3. Confirm the deactivation.

Deactivated users cannot log in, but their historical data (contact logs, SPMs, audit trail) is preserved.

> ⚠️ **Before deactivating:** Reassign their clients to an active planner to avoid orphaned records.

### Resetting a User's Password

If a user is locked out:
1. In the Admin Panel, find the user.
2. Click **Send Password Reset** → confirm.

The user will receive a reset email. They must complete the reset within 2 hours.

### Managing Team Assignments

1. In the Admin Panel, find the user.
2. Click **Edit Team Assignment**.
3. Select the correct team from the dropdown.
4. Click **Save**.

## 9. Audit Log — HIPAA Compliance

The Audit Log records every significant action taken in CaseSync. It is your primary tool for HIPAA compliance review and incident investigation.

### Accessing the Audit Log

Click **Audit Log** in the top navigation bar.

### What the Audit Log Records

Every entry in the Audit Log includes:
- **Timestamp** — exact date and time of the action (UTC)
- **User** — who performed the action
- **Action** — what was done (e.g., "Viewed client profile," "Updated contact log," "Exported report")
- **Resource** — what was affected (client ID, document name, etc.)
- **IP Address** — where the action originated

### Types of Actions Logged

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Authentication** | Login, logout, failed login attempt, password reset |
| **Client Records** | Viewed profile, edited field, deleted entry |
| **Contact Logs** | Created, edited, deleted contact log entry |
| **SPMs** | Submitted, edited, deleted SPM |
| **Documents** | Uploaded, downloaded, deleted document |
| **Admin Actions** | Invited user, changed role, deactivated account |
| **Exports** | Downloaded CSV report |
| **Chat** | (Message content is not logged, but session activity is) |

### Filtering the Audit Log

Use the filter options to narrow results:
- **Date range** — search within specific dates
- **User** — see all actions by a specific person
- **Action type** — filter by category (e.g., only "Client Records")
- **Client** — see all actions related to a specific client

### Investigating an Incident

If you need to investigate a potential HIPAA incident (unauthorized access, accidental disclosure):
1. Open the Audit Log.
2. Filter by the date of the incident.
3. Search for the client or user involved.
4. Review the list of actions — look for unusual access patterns (e.g., a user accessing many records quickly, or accessing records outside their caseload).
5. Export the relevant log entries as CSV for your records.

### Exporting the Audit Log

1. Apply your filters.
2. Click **Export to CSV**.
3. The file will download to your computer.

> ⚠️ **Reminder:** Audit log exports contain sensitive data. Store them securely and in compliance with BLH's data retention policy. Do not share audit logs via email without encryption.

### HIPAA Minimum Necessary Standard

When reviewing audit logs, remember the HIPAA Minimum Necessary rule: users should only access the client records necessary for their job. If you see a Supports Planner regularly accessing records outside their assigned caseload, investigate and document your findings.

## 10. Org-Wide Reporting & CSV Export

CaseSync includes reporting tools to help you monitor compliance, prepare for audits, and make data-driven staffing decisions.

### Accessing Reports

From the **Supervisor Dashboard**, click the **Reports** tab.

### Available Report Types

| Report | Description |
|---|---|
| **SPM Compliance Report** | All SPMs submitted (or not) by month, team, and planner |
| **Overdue Deadlines Report** | All overdue items across the org, with planner and team |
| **Client Risk Report** | All clients ranked by risk score |
| **Contact Log Activity** | Contact log volume by planner and time period |
| **Service Authorization Expiry** | Clients with authorizations expiring in the next 30/60/90 days |
| **User Activity Report** | Login frequency and last-active dates per user |

### Generating a Report

1. In the Reports tab, select the report type.
2. Set your filters:
   - **Date range** — required for most reports
   - **Team** — leave blank for org-wide, or select a specific team
   - **Planner** — optional, for individual-level reports
3. Click **Generate Report**.
4. The report will render on-screen with interactive charts and a data table.

### Exporting to CSV

1. After generating a report, click **Export to CSV**.
2. The file will download to your computer.
3. Open in Excel or Google Sheets for further analysis or formatting.

> 💡 **Tip:** For state agency reporting, generate the SPM Compliance Report and Service Authorization Expiry report on the 16th of each month. This gives you a clean picture of the prior month's activity plus upcoming renewals.

### Scheduling Reports (if available)

Some reports can be scheduled for automatic delivery:
1. Generate the report.
2. Click **Schedule This Report**.
3. Choose frequency (monthly, weekly) and delivery time.
4. The report will be saved to your Supervisor Dashboard automatically.

### Using Reports for Supervision

| Report | Use It To... |
|---|---|
| SPM Compliance | Identify which teams and planners need follow-up |
| Overdue Deadlines | Prioritize your supervisory calls this week |
| Client Risk | Find high-risk clients for quality review visits |
| Service Auth Expiry | Proactively manage renewals before services lapse |
| User Activity | Identify inactive users who may have left the org |

### Key Dates

| Date | Action |
|---|---|
| 15th of month | SPM deadline — all planners must have submitted |
| 16th of month | Review compliance reports, follow up with TMs |
| 1st of month | Review prior month summary, plan current month |
| Ongoing | Monitor Audit Log for unusual activity |

### Common Issues & Solutions

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| User can't log in | Send password reset from Admin Panel |
| Client in wrong team | Edit client profile, change assigned planner |
| Missing SPMs in report | Check that submissions were finalized (not just saved as draft) |
| User's role shows incorrectly | Update role in Admin Panel; user should refresh browser |
| Audit log not showing recent actions | Actions can take a few minutes to appear; refresh the page |
| CSV export is empty | Check that your date range and filters include the expected data |

### HIPAA Reminders

- Review the Audit Log **monthly** for unusual access patterns.
- **Deactivate** accounts promptly when staff leave.
- Conduct **annual access reviews** — ensure all active users still need their level of access.
- Document any suspected HIPAA incidents per BLH's incident response policy.
- Audit log exports are considered **PHI** — handle them accordingly.
